# Design

The visual system of soumyajyoti-portfolio: a cozy, flat-vector road-trip strip wrapped around a warm, readable resume. UI tokens live in `css/style.css` (`:root` + night overrides); the scene is generated by `js/scene.js` and colored entirely through `--sc-*` CSS variables.

## Theme: day drive / night drive

Two full themes, and they are diegetic: **light = day drive** (sun, drifting clouds), **dark = night drive** (moon, stars, fireflies, windows glowing, headlight beam on). Theme resolves as: manual toggle (`data-theme` on `<html>`, persisted in localStorage, applied pre-paint by an inline head script) → falls back to `prefers-color-scheme`. Every dark override is written twice — once under the media query with `:root:not([data-theme="light"])`, once under `:root[data-theme="dark"]` — so both signals win in both directions. `.day-only` / `.night-only` utility classes gate scene décor.

## Color

Day UI: sky-tinted paper `oklch(0.972 0.012 225)`, blue-slate ink, coral brand `oklch(0.55 0.155 38)` (the car), open-to-work green, sun-yellow for awards only. Night UI: deep navy `oklch(0.21 0.03 255)` bg, light ink, coral brightened to `oklch(0.72 0.15 40)` with **dark text on coral buttons** (`--on-brand` flips). Scene palette (`--sc-*`): pastoral greens, tan road, gray mountains with snow, warm signboards — each with a moonlit night variant. Strategy: Full palette in the scene, quiet chrome around it.

## Typography

Baloo 2 (600/700/800) for display, buttons, chips; Nunito Sans for body at 1.0625rem/1.65. Fluid `clamp()` headings, `text-wrap: balance`, emoji-led section headings (🧭 🏔️ 🌲 📄 🎓) as the wayfinding system — no eyebrow labels.

## The journey (signature element)

A scroll-driven side-view drive: `#journey-track` is a tall scroll region; `.journey-stage` sticks at `100svh`; three SVG layers parallax past a fixed car (factors 0.22 / 0.55 / 1). Scroll distance maps 1:1 to road distance; wheels rotate from scroll delta; checkpoints (from `js/content.js`, positions from `CP_START + i × CP_SPACING` in scene.js) trigger their card within ±300 world units. A mini-map pill tracks progress; visiting all seven fires confetti and the completion banner. The whole scene scales down on short viewports via `#scene-scale` (world coordinates divide by the scale factor). Reduced motion: clouds/stars/fireflies/bob/confetti off; the scroll mapping itself remains, being user-driven.

## Shape, depth, motion

16px radii, pill buttons/chips (44px min targets), soft blue-tinted two-level shadows, one dashed-coral border on the drive invite (route motif, used once). Ease `cubic-bezier(0.22,1,0.36,1)`, no bounce. Ambient motion is scenery-only (clouds drift, stars twinkle, fireflies float); everything else is scroll-mapped.

## Components

- **Checkpoint card** (`.zone-card`): white/dark panel, right side on desktop, bottom sheet on mobile; appears/disappears with proximity, no close button — driving away dismisses it.
- **Mini-map** (`.journey-map`): dashed line, 7 emoji dots (grayscale until visited, green ring when visited, scaled when current), car emoji riding the line.
- **Project cards, experience rows, teaching entries**: unchanged warm system, all token-driven so they theme automatically.

## Testing hooks (main.js)

`?pin=0.42` freezes the journey at a progress with hero/topbar hidden (headless screenshots — sticky doesn't re-resolve under programmatic scroll in headless). `?theme=light|dark` forces a theme. `?p=0.42` deep-links by actually scrolling.
